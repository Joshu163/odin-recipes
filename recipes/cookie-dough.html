Edible Cookie Dough

Authentic Mexican Carne Asada marinated in citrus juices, beer, and spices, and then grilled over hot coals for a wonderful charred flavor. This delicious Mexican entree is wonderful with rice and beans and equally amazing in burritos, tacos, quesadillas, and nachos.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoon milk
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. To heat-treat your flour so it is safe to use: Place flour in a microwave safe dish and cook for 1 minitue and 15 seconds, stirring it every 15 seconds. Set aside.
  2. Beat sugar and butter with an electric mixer in a large bowl until creamy. Beat in vanilla extract and salt. Add the heat treated flour; mix until crumbly dough forms. Stir in milk until dough is just combined; fold in milk chocolat4e chips and mini chocolate chips.
